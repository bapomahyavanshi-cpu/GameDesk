Technical Rules & Gameplay

  • At least 2 players must record full POV.
  • Screenshot of final match result is mandatory.
  • Flare guns are strictly prohibited.
  • Emergency backpack is not allowed.
  • Only in-game voice chat should be used.

Fair Play & Conduct

  • No hacking, mods or third-party apps.
  • No teaming with other squads.
  • No abusive language or toxic behaviour.
  • No stream sniping or ghosting.

Punctuality & Scheduling

  • Teams must check-in 10 minutes before match.
  • Players must join lobby 5 minutes before start.
  • Failure to join within 5 minutes = Walkover.

Match Remake Policy

  • More than 32 players experience server lag.
  • Technical issue within first 3 minutes.
  • Plane does not start or players cannot move.

Lobby Rules

  • Room ID & Password will be shared 10 minutes before match.
  • Players must join the room on time.
  • Teams must stay in their assigned slot.
  • Changing slot without admin permission is not allowed.

Internet & Device Responsibility

  • Players are responsible for their own internet connection.
  • Matches will not restart due to personal internet issues.
  • Players must use mobile devices only.

Player Identity

  • Players must use the same in-game name used during registration.
  • Account sharing is strictly prohibited.
  • Tournament admins may request identity verification.

Streaming Rules

  • Players may stream matches with minimum 5 minute delay.
  • Stream sniping will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Unauthorized live streaming is not allowed.

Tie Breaker Rules

  • Higher total kills.
  • Higher placement in a single match.
  • Better overall performance in tournament.
  • Admin decision will be final.

Disqualification

  • Use of hacks or cheats.
  • Using prohibited items such as flare gun.
  • Misconduct or toxic behaviour.
  • Breaking tournament rules.

Prizes & Distribution

Admin Authority

  • Tournament admins have full authority over match decisions.
  • Admins may modify rules if required for fair play.
  • Admin decisions will be final and cannot be challenged.

Slot Ownership

  • Teams must occupy their assigned slot numbers.
  • Changing slots without permission may result in penalties.
  • Admins may remove teams from incorrect slots.

Spectator Policy

  • Only authorized spectators or admins are allowed in the room.
  • Unauthorized spectators may lead to match restart.
  • Players must not invite spectators to custom rooms.

Result Submission

  • Team leaders must submit final match screenshots.
  • Screenshots must clearly show kills and placement.
  • Fake or edited screenshots will lead to disqualification.

Protest & Disputes

  • Teams can raise disputes within 15 minutes after match.
  • Proof such as POV recording or screenshots must be provided.
  • Admins will review the evidence and make the final decision.

Code of Conduct

  • Players must respect other participants and admins.
  • Racism, harassment or threats will result in bans.
  • Players must maintain sportsmanship during matches.
