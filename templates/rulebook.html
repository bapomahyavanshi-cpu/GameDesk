BGMI

Official Competition Rulebook

Version 1.0.0
December 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This rulebook defines the official rules and regulations for BGMI tournaments organized by Game Desk Esports. All participants must read and agree to these rules before registering for any tournament.

2. Player Eligibility

  • Players must be at least 16 years old.
  • Only Indian residents are allowed to participate.
  • BGMI account level must be 50+.
  • Use of emulator or hacks is strictly prohibited.

3. Team Rules

  • Each team must have exactly 4 main players.
  • One substitute player is allowed.
  • Team name must be unique and appropriate.
  • Roster changes after registration are not allowed.

4. Match Rules

  • Game Mode: Squad (TPP)
  • Maps: Erangel / Miramar
  • Room ID & Password will be shared before match.
  • Late entry will result in point deduction.

5. Fair Play & Conduct

  • No cheating, teaming, or use of third-party tools.
  • No abusive language or toxic behavior.
  • All decisions by tournament admins are final.
  • Violation may lead to disqualification or ban.

6. Tournament Prizes

  • Total Prize Pool: ₹10,00,000
  • 1st Place – ₹5,00,000
  • 2nd Place – ₹2,50,000
  • 3rd Place – ₹1,50,000
  • MVP Player – ₹1,00,000

7. Disqualification

  • Use of hacks or exploits.
  • Sharing room credentials.
  • Fake information during registration.

8. Organizer Rights

Game Desk Esports reserves the right to modify rules, cancel matches, or take disciplinary action if required.

This rulebook is created for academic and demonstration purposes as part of the Game Desk Esports project.
